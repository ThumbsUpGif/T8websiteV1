Occasions & Deadlines

A minimal countdown timer keeping you motivated and focused on the big day ahead of you.

ABOUT

Sleek and minimal, the Occasions & Deadlines app is designed to do its job as unobtrusively as possible and stay in the background of your workflow. At the same time, as a dedicated app it keeps your attention focused on the most important date ahead of you and nudges you to plan the rest of your time accordingly. Be it your exam, deadline, birthday or holiday, this app lets you count down the days, hours, minutes and seconds that remain. A handy reference for your scheduling, and a great way to cheer you up if you have something exciting planned.
